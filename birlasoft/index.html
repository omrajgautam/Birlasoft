Birlasoft [BSE: 532400, NSE: BSOFT] is a leading $500mn publicly listed Enterprise Digital and IT Services company
As part of the multibillion dollar diversified The CK Birla Group, Birlasoft with its 10,000 employees, is committed to continuing our 159-year heritage of building sustainable communities. Birlasoft combines the power of domain, enterprise and digital technologies to reimagine business processes for customers and their ecosystem. Its consultative and design thinking approach makes societies more productive by helping customers run businesses.
