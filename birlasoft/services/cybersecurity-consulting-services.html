Conventional manual-led cybersecurity risk assessments are ineffective, expensive, and tedious to manage
As cybersecurity threats increase exponentially, so do the number and extent of the regulations that seek to protect organizations and their customers. Countering this challenge with spending thousands of person-hours to complete cyber security self-assessments, swamping yourself with lengthy supplier risk assessments, and burning out your workforce with mundane activities can damage your profits and enterprise security.
Complying with this increasing compliance burden manually is not an option anymore. Your responses will be expensive, overdue, and out of date.
Regulated institutions are critical targets for cybercriminals and must navigate both an increasing number and a convoluted system of regulations and standards. Choosing a manual approach to counter this threat makes you more vulnerable.
Transform your cybersecurity regulatory compliance
Let’s put AI and automation to work
Regulativ.ai and Birlasoft join forces to automate your cyber security regulatory compliance
Helping you achieve super-fast, accurate, consistent and, cost-effective compliance
How Does It Work?
A cloud-based solution that uses AI, machine learning, automation, and NLP to transform your cyber risk regulatory compliance. It interprets the answers to self-assessment and supplier risk security assessments and automates the responses using stored data from previous exercises and new data sourced internally from organizational RFIs.
