Enterprise Technology Services and Platforms
Exponential value from technology investments
Technology is evolving at a rapid pace, offering incredible opportunities for Enterprises IT to strategically invest in technology platforms with a keen focus on value generation.
By selecting the best-fit technology solutions in-sync with near and long-term objectives, enterprises can use technology as a key business lever. Birlasoft partners with leading technology providers around the globe, to help clients achieve their digital transformation goals. We optimize platform configurations to unlock maximum value generation possibilities, reducing in-house IT effort and preparing the landscape for future transformation.
Birlasoft works with companies in diverse sectors including Automotive, Banking, Insurance, Manufacturing, Media and Entertainment, Life Sciences, and Energy and Resources. Across these sectors, our alliances with technology leaders help us to accelerate digital transformation and generate value.
  • Oracle and JD Edwards
    Our solutions for Oracle’s technology suite cover JD Edwards, Fusion Middleware, Business intelligence, Logistics, and Compliance, among others. This is bolstered by a rich set of IP built on the Oracle platform. We are an Oracle Platinum partner, with 20 specializations, and recognized as a niche player in the Gartner 2019 Magic Quadrant for Cloud Applications worldwide.
    Read more about our Oracle Cloud Service offerings here
  • SAP
    Birlasoft has partnered with SAP to bring clients productized solutions and rapid deployment services. We leverage pre-configured best practices and project accelerator tools, to speed up time-to-value. Our competencies cover the entire SAP portfolio, owing to our position as an SAP Gold Partner, and the recipient of the SAP® North America Partner Excellence Award 2019.
    More about our SAP offerings and team of experts here
  • Infor
    Birlasoft employs 300+ certified Infor experts, to streamline adoption via consulting, implementation, migration, extension, and infrastructure management services. Popular Infor solutions covered by Birlasoft include M3, LM, XA, EAM, and CloudSuite. With over 20+ years of Infor experience, and an Offshore Development Center (ODC) dedicated to this technology, we help clients take informed decisions and enhance their business value.
    More about our service offerings here
  • Microsoft
    Birlasoft partners with Microsoft to drive transformation across key enterprise areas like business intelligence, analytics, ERP, mobility, CRM, and the cloud. This is backed by extensive knowledge of Microsoft Dynamics, Azure, and SharePoint. We are a Microsoft Gold Certified Partner, with extensive customization capabilities for Microsoft enterprise technology.
    Read more about our service offerings here
Enterprise Services
Birlasoft is well poised for targeted transformations in mission-critical business scenarios. We recognize how technology can drive value in key areas of operation across, process execution, product management, marketing and the distribution/supply chain. Our partnerships with technology giants help us to offer comprehensive enterprise services.
  • Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
    For both B2B and B2C enterprises, CRM plays a vital role in revenue generation and business growth. Birlasoft offers CRM services for Salesforce, Microsoft, SAP, and Oracle along with a full suite of advisory solutions. Our cross-industry capabilities earned us the "Rising Star USA in the Salesforce Ecosystem" rank by ISG.
    More about our CRM Services here
  • Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES)
    A cutting-edge MES platform can revolutionize productivity for the industrial sector, impacting production, labor utilization, and warehouse maximization. Birlasoft helps to unlock tangible business benefits from MES investments, eliminating paper processes and driving digital transformation.
    More about our MES Offerings here
  • Product Lifecycle Management (PLM)
    With product lifecycle becoming more complex, enterprises require an agile innovation process, powered by data. Birlasoft has partnered with PTC, Siemens, Oracle, and IBM to help enterprises modernize their PLM approach.
    Learn more about our PLM services and products portfolios here
  • Supply Chain Management (SCM)
    An agile and efficient supply chain could be a competitive differentiator for modern enterprises. Birlasoft’s services for transportation, warehouse, and global trade management helps to unlock latent supply chain opportunities in a dynamic market. Our service capabilities span the entire breadth of supply chain requirements, aided by our partnerships with Oracle and SAP.
    More about our SCM Services portfolio here
  • IT Transformation

    More about our IT Transformation Services here
