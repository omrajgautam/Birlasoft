Customer Highlights
Enabling safer RMX operations, and driving customer excellence and process efficiency with award-winning IoT and Analytics platform
A global building materials company dealing in Ready-Mix Cement (RMX) was looking to improve customer satisfaction, accurately forecast demands, improve operational efficiency, and reduce costs related to logistics and transportation of RMX to their customer's location.
Empowering the Channel Partners by leveraging the power of Cloud and Mobile
APAC's leading cement manufacturer was looking to elevate the experience of its channel partners i.e. the dealers, retailers and influencers, and enable real-time visibility across the channel. The manufacturer wanted to remove dependency of its partners on its back-office team for operational tasks such as order booking, finance, reporting, etc., Read more which was a very manual and time-consuming process. Birlasoft recommended a mobile-first approach to the customer and developed a scalable, next-generation platform on AWS that empowers the channel partners to perform a whole lot of activities directly from a mobile app or a web portal. The channel partners can now place orders for cement, check their credit limit, access sales reports, etc., anytime and from anywhere. Birlasoft leveraged its design thinking approach, and customer experience and next-gen development capabilities to develop the solution, thereby providing a uniform experience to all dealers, retailers, and influencers across any channel. In future, this platform will be extended to the channel partners of other product lines of the manufacturer and will have multi-lingual capabilities to support multiple regional languages to make it easy for channels partners to use the app and web portal & stay connected with the manufacturer. Read less
Improving operational efficiency and reducing costs for a leading US based Airline
A leading US based airline partnered with Birlasoft to develop its next-generation, cloud-based platform to digitize paper-based Cargo Load Report. The eCLR (Electronic Cargo Load Report) platform, developed by Birlasoft, has helped the airline to improve flight operational efficiency (e.g. Minimum Service Time performance), Read more ensure better synergy between staff, achieve significant cost savings, and improve customer experience. The platform is used by the airline ground staff to transfer information (related to passengers, bags and cargo) in real-time for accurate and timely weight and balance performance calculation, thereby removing need for human intervention, avoiding FAA fines, and eliminating inefficient and costly paper retention process. The platform, built on AWS, integrates multiple applications to pull high-volume and high-frequency real-time events' data to provide relevant and timely information to the staff, and is built for high performance, availability, and scalability. Read less
Elevating Customer and Employee Experience for a leading US based Airline
A leading US based airline company partnered with Birlasoft to elevate customer and employee experience by developing an industry-first platform for digitizing Hotel Accommodation Process. The Hotel Accommodation platform automates the process of hotel booking & payments during IRROPs (Irregular Operations).
Read more This platform, built on AWS, provides the airline staff and agents better visibility into hotel inventory, enables them to timely deliver offers, hotel booking information and confirmations to passengers and make hotel payments. As a result, the airline was able to decrease wait lines by 50% for hotel vouchers, saw a 44% decrease in average processing time per flight, and improved customer and agent satisfaction.

Predictive Maintenance for European Manufacturer
Birlasoft has developed predictive models to help increase the uptime levels for a leading global manufacturer of textile machines. Birlasoft''s Big Data platform processes near real-time sensor data and employs predictive analytics to enable the development of predictive models. These models help forecast machine failures before they occur, and reduce the unplanned downtime by 50%.

We leveraged our digital consulting and design thinking led product development approach, to understand their needs and develop this platform.
