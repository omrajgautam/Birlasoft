Birlasoft's IT Transformation Services (ITTS) offers an integrated (advise to execute) approach towards business process, technology and operating model transformation. Our objective is to enable enhanced business-IT alignment, realize business value, optimize TCO (Total Cost of Ownership) for IT Assets and Services.
 
Our services begin with designing the ambition, the transformation plan, and prioritization and we ensure a seamless execution to achieve the objective and goals.
Our Practice Portfolio includes
a) IT Consulting and Business Analysis
b) IT Governance
c) Program Management and PMO Services
d) API Management, Integration and Middleware
 
With experience across a wide range of capability areas, our consultants help drive Strategy and business case creation and formulate execution plans to support overall benefits realization. Strong capabilities across established and disruptive application technologies combined with the right IPs and frameworks, help reduce cost, time and risk while executing transformation plans.
Our strong methodologies/frameworks are automated through our proprietary web based tool - 3rdEye™
3rdEyeTM

3rdEyeTM is our propriety web based tool for enabling IT Portfolio Transformation by providing a structured framework for capturing and analyzing IT Service and Asset information. 3rdEye™ can be used on a continuous basis by IT organizations to manage IT services and assets. It helps in assessing various IT Assets like applications, infrastructure, databases etc. with respect to their utilization, support to business, technical complexity, cost of ownership (TCO), fitness/suitability for business purpose etc. The analysis engine in the tool does processing of the data gathered and provides actionable intelligence to IT management which helps them in defining their future roadmap.

which offers a structured way to capture and analyze relevant information.
Practice Portfolio
 
IT Consulting and Business Analysis
Explore
The Digital age presents several challenges and opportunities for providing Advisory, Consulting and Business Analysis as services to help customers realize Business and Technology value.
Organizations with a large and complex IT portfolio would typically have several opportunities for optimization in terms of:
  • Duplicate applications
  • Obsolete technology
  • Functional gaps
  • Integration gaps
  • Non-standardized platforms & processes
These portfolio inefficiencies and redundancies create 'Technology Debt' which often hampers IT's ability to optimally support current and emerging business opportunities and a higher Total Cost of Ownership for IT portfolio. Birlasoft leverages its proprietary ZeROTechDebt™ framework which focuses on optimizing an organization's 'Technology Debt'.
The framework specifically aims for Zero Redundancy Operations to attain Cost Leadership and business alignment. Birlasoft has a proven methodology 'EASE- Explore, Analyze, Strategize, Execute' for driving various Transformation programs.
The areas of focus include Application Portfolio Optimization (APO) and IT Strategy, Business Process Re-engineering and Product Selection, IT M&A Consulting, Portfolio Simplification – Decommissioning, Value Discovery & Value Appropriation, SIAM– Service Integration and Management & Operating Model and CoE Setup.
 
Capabilites
 
IT Merger Advisory
Organizations have been taking the Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A) route to grow, compete and meet ever-increasing regulatory requirements. Birlasoft has a suit of offerings across the length and breadth of the merger process – due diligence, pre-integration, integration, post-integration and steady state to bring synergies across the organizations. Our teams have developed a set of robust playbooks through our extensive experience which enables a standardized approach for M&A IT Integration, including detailed processes, activities, inputs, dependencies, templates and timelines required to carry out integration activities.
Application Decommissioning Advisory
Our Decommissioning Framework is a step-by-step guide for decommissioning planning and execution. Birlasoft has assisted customers in developing a transformational roadmap and systematic execution plans that include application consolidation/retirement, data archival, new solution rollout, data migration to new systems and adjusting underlying infrastructure. Birlasoft has defined an end-to-end retirement execution process along with standard templates, checklists, process, activities and approval workflows required in the decommissioning process.
IT Strategy and Value Discovery
Business-IT alignment is the key to reducing IT operating costs and prioritizing projects with minimal risk to the business.IT Strategy service is focused on creating a business aligned technology strategy and roadmap - this is enabled by means of the IT Value Discovery framework which is a broad based assessment across a number of parameters. The objective is to assess the IT landscape, define a business aligned IT strategy, and create a strategic roadmap to achieve IT objectives.
Business Process Re-engineering Advisory
Birlasoft has rich experience in the business process mapping and re-engineering space. Our intent through this offering is to align current state processes to industry best practices and bring operational performance improvements. We drive this through increased visibility, standardization, intelligent automation, enhanced and increased consistency of customer experience, user experience and elimination of redundant activities and bottlenecks. The end goal is to enable the organization to implement business strategies with reduced time and effort.
Application Portfolio Optimization Advisory
Application Portfolio Analysis enables portfolio optimization from the perspective of business alignment and TCO. This would include defining the target state of the portfolio and the roadmap to reach the target state. To reduce operational costs and free up technical debt, there is a need to reduce the number of applications and amount of code by consolidating applications, retiring or decommissioning under-utilized applications, and driving standardization into common applications. A structured approach of Application Portfolio Analysis is used to rationalize the IT applications and assets of the organization and generates a program plan which includes initiatives identified to optimize client's IT setup, long term roadmap, skills needed, sourcing options, investment, risks and mitigation plan.

Explore
Deals with implementation of Enterprise Service Management products and platforms. Adopting a People/Process/Tools centric approach, Birlasoft offers consulting led implementation services with a strong training program for customers. The platforms we have expertise in are ServiceNow®
ServiceNow®

ServiceNow® offers a portfolio of robust cloud-based applications (Enterprise and Express Editions) that automate and manage enterprise services. The applications have the advantage of being built on a single service automation platform with one user interface, one code base, and one data model, delivering easy, automated upgrades. ServiceNow ISO 27001 certified cloud platform with 99.8% availability is offered globally to customers via high availability mirrored data centers. The ServiceNow® applications are broadly classified into five product suites: IT Service Automation, Project Portfolio, IT Business Management, ESM Single Apps and Development Suite.

The platform system is inclusive of the below components:

  • IT Service Automation

    Incident Management: Enables Customer to restore normal IT operations by providing capabilities to record, classify, distribute, and manage incidents through to resolution. Includes Performance Analytics limited to 15 KPIs, 1 dashboard and 90 days of data captured by Incident Management.

    Problem Management: Facilitates the process of identifying the root causes of errors in the IT infrastructure by providing capabilities to record, escalate, and manage problems through to resolution.

    Release Management: Facilitates the planning, design, build, configuration, testing, and release of hardware and software into the IT infrastructure.

    Asset Management: Provides capabilities to track and manage the physical, contractual, and financial aspects of IT assets.

    Request Management: Provides capabilities to approve and fulfill requests for IT goods and services defined and presented in the service catalog.

    IT Cost Management: Provides capabilities to track one-time and recurring costs of configuration items used by IT and allocate those costs to business units using allocation rules.

    Configuration Management (CMDB): Provides capabilities to identify, record, and report on IT configuration items and their relationships.

  • Project Portfolio

    Project Portfolio: Provides capabilities to plan, organize, and manage IT projects and project portfolios including associated tasks and resources.

    Resource: Provides a view of IT projects and the availability, allocation, and capacity of assigned IT resources.

    Demand: Consolidates IT requests through a service catalog and routes them in a workflow to stakeholders who gather additional information to prioritize investment decisions.

    SDLC: Provides capabilities to manage the software development process in IT projects including enhancement requests, defect prioritization, definition of release content, and tasks.

  • IT Business Management

    IT GRC: Provides capabilities to document IT policies and procedures, define and assess risks and controls, audit and test controls, and track remediation tasks.

    Vendor: Enables Customer to manage, evaluate and compare IT vendors based on predefined criteria.

  • ESM Single Apps

    HR: Provides capabilities to manage the service delivery of a human resource department by offering a self-service catalog and assignment of requests based on fulfillment rules.

    Facilities: Provides capabilities to manage the service delivery of a facilities department by offering a self-service catalog and assignment of requests based on fulfillment rules.

    Field Services: Provides capabilities to create work order records for the repair and service of equipment. Requires IT Service Automation Suite.

  • Development Suite

    App Creator: Allows Customer to build Custom Applications on top of the Service Automation Platform.
