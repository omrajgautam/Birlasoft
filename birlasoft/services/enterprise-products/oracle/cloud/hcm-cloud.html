Oracle HCM Cloud

Create an Engaging Employee Experience Using HCM Cloud Applications

With a dynamically changing work environment, dramatic transformation in the HR industry, and an ever-increasing young workforce, human capital management has become a more strategic focus in organizations. Human resource challenges like workforce planning, Talent acquisition, retention, succession planning and skill gaps can be addressed through a HCM solution, which needs to play a key role at every stage of employee engagement: from recruitment and onboarding through development and alignment of performance and goals to retirement.
HCM Cloud offers a simple yet powerful solution to transform your organization and get the best out of your workforce. Birlasoft covers multiple functionalities across HCM implementation and support such as core HR, talent, absence, payroll/payroll interface, compensation, benefits, HDL, PBL, FBL, SDL, extracts, OTBI, and BIP, etc. Birlasoft Oracle Cloud advisory services provide its customers with an optimized roadmap for the Cloud adoption program.
HR Transformation Enabled by Oracle HCM Cloud
 
Integrated HR data and processes
HR Organizations are transforming and expanding their horizon from administrative tasks and records keeping to providing more strategic value. Easy access of information through structured processed data for associates enable the HRO for richer associate engagement. The right HCM solutions provides the right tools and inputs for strategic decision making while reducing the effort for administrative tasks around data entry and tracking.
Workforce Empowerment
The industry challenges around Talent hiring and reward is increasingly being addressed through use of right HCM solutions. Top innovation practices in Modern HR are increasingly using analytics, mobility solutions and social media to improve employee engagement and performance. Oracle HCM cloud provides a flexible, configurable and modern HCM Cloud global system that has in built Social, Mobile and Analytics features.
 
 
Global Mobility
Oracle HCM Cloud brings the solution where you can make it easy for everyone to connect on any device, search, develop, and preserve the best endowment, enable cooperation, provide thorough workforce comprehensions and as well as increase operational efficiency.
Digital Transformation
Cloud computing provides a way to migrate legacy systems into a more adaptable, agile environment and allows organizations to take help of new developing technologies such as mobile, integration, deep insights using analytics and the Internet of Things.
