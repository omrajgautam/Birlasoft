Accounts Payable Automation for JD Edwards 
Accounts payable is a costly and time-consuming process with heavy reliance on paper. Oracle and Birlasoft have partnered to create a best-of-breed solution for JD Edwards users to automate invoice processing. Built on the industry-leading WebCenter Content Management Platform, the solution leverages WebCenter Imaging along with WebCenter Capture and WebCenter Forms Recognition, to provide robust document capture, advanced imaging and workflow capabilities and seamless delivery of critical business information to JD Edwards resulting in an end-to-end (capture to archive) invoice processing solution.

Birlasoft JD Edwards Adapter for Oracle WebCenter (OVI Certified)
Birlasoft's JD Edwards Adapter for WebCenter enables business users to easily access, view and initiate tasks on documents associated with transactions directly from within or outside the JD Edwards ERP environment. By leveraging Oracle WebCenter's open content management platform, the adapter facilitates the development of document-driven solutions that increase the efficiency of line-of-business operations while providing a scalable and consolidated enterprise-class Fusion Middleware infrastructure to content-enable applications across the enterprise.
Key Benefits
  • Out-of-the-box, standards-based integration with JD Edwards
  • Streamlines operations with in-context document management for JD Edwards users while providing document review access for users outside the system
  • Consolidates file servers by storing all JD Edwards and other application content-related documents in a scalable and secure unified repository
  • Increases visibility across invoice processing transactions and comprehensively audits trails of all actions taken within the process.

Application-to-Application Integrations

Oracle SOA Suite (B2B) for 3PL Integration
Birlasoft's Oracle SOA Suite (B2B) for 3PL Integration enables manufacturing organizations to focus on their core business propositions and leverage outsourced warehousing and distribution services. It integrates with the customer's ERP system and allows them to use the 3PL warehouse by extending the ERP availability to the warehouse location.
Key Objectives
  • Seamlessly integrate the ERP business processes with external logistics and warehousing provider
  • User experience for the customer remains the same whether it is while working with their own warehouse or while working with a 3PL's warehouse
  • Automated Ship confirmation in ERP when the 3PL warehouse sends out the shipment to end customer
  • Purchase Order processing using the 3PL's warehouse for supplier deliveries
  • Purchase Order Receipt processing in ERP even when using the 3PL's warehouse for receipt
  • Real time inventory synchronization between 3PL's warehouse system and ERP inventory module
Key Benefits
  • Oracle B2B platform allows communicating with the 3PL provider using industry protocols such as EDI, RosettaNet, ebXML, cXML or custom formats
  • Ability to leverage same or different 3PL providers in different regions where the organization wants to work
  • Easy rolling out of new 3PL providers.
  • High user productivity due to the seamless integration of ERP systems with the 3PL provider's IT systems
  • One common platform for A2A and B2B transactions
