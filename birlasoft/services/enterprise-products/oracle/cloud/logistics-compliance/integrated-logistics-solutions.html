Reimagine Logistics to Meet Business Demand.
Transform your logistics processes, build digital agility to deliver customer delight, improve cost and revenue and strategically manage supply chain assets with Birlasoft's future-ready Integrated Logistics Solutions.
 
Birlasoft brings the right combination of industry experience and Oracle knowledge to deliver hassle-free logistics solutions. Birlasoft's Integrated Logistics Solutions are designed to streamline supply chain process and help Freight Forwarding, Contract Logistics, NVOCC/Liners, Ship Owning and Operations companies experience value realization through digital transformation.

The volatility in the business environment, the advent of new technologies, and the uncertain global economy impact the logistics industry. Today's competitive logistics business is all about delivering greater customer value at a lower cost while managing razor-thin margins.
The modern dynamic supply chain poses unique challenges for logistics solution providers, Freight Forwarders, NVOCC/Liners, and Ship Owning and Operations who need fast, cost-effective solutions to manage service levels.
Challenges Across Logistics Verticals
Contract Logistics
  • Optimal load, route planning
  • Optimal utilization of warehouses
  • Task and resource planning
  • Manpower planning in warehouse
  • Enabling barcode based transactions
  • Managing value added services
  • Fleet maintenance
  • Driver management
  • Dry and cold warehousing
  • Collaboration with external service providers
  • Fleet monitoring
Freight Forwarding
  • Profitability per transaction
  • Duplication of data entry
  • Credit control
  • Collaboration between shipper & consignee
  • Compliance to taxation
  • Buyers consolidation
  • Central buying and procurement of services (Aggregation and volume discount within group)
  • Simulation of route/mode planning to meet required due dates from customer
NVOCC/Liners
  • Container tracking inventory management
  • Container tracking and placement based on annual plan
  • What if scenario planning
  • Capturing all cost elements across legs
  • Turnaround time with in time lock
  • Container maintenance and repairs
  • Minimize empty container movement
  • Operational tasks and visibility for agencies
  • Management of GSA for agents
  • Detention and demurrages
Ship Owning and Operations
  • Managing process life cycle for JV, slot swap/purchase, independent operations
  • Cost, revenue and operational tracking for each voyage – vessel voyage profitability
  • Management of GSA for agents
  • Optimal utilization of available capacity/slots
  • Effective rate management for domestic (Per Ton) and EXIM (Per TeU)
  • Collaborations with inhouse and external agencies in various sectors
