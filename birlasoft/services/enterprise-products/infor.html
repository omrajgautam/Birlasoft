Expertise
With multiple competencies, CoE's and experience across different industries, Birlasoft's Infor practice provides holistic expertise the range of Infor products.
Infor CloudSuite (M3)
CloudSuite Distribution Enterprise, Food and Beverages, Manufacturing, Chemical and Fashion. Birlasoft's Infor M3 practice offers an extensive range of solutions and services encompassing design, development, implementation, upgrades, integration, global roll-outs and more across Infor M3 CloudSuite.
Infor LN and BaaN
Infor LN is the next generation ERP system that is specialized by industry and optimized for complex, global operations. Birlasoft's Infor LN/BaaN expertise is spread across implementations, integration and optimization of BaaN and Infor ERP products, to automation, country-specific localization, and maintenance, among others.
Infor CloudSuite™ Industrial / SyteLine
Birlasoft offers a wide range of competitive Infor CSI services across all modules of CSI. Birlasoft is amongst the only few Infor partners globally with the capability to successfully deliver & complete an end-to-end Implementation in less than 8 weeks.
Infor XA and Mapics
Our Infor XA group of experts and specialists bring their expertise from across different versions and domains, to our Infor XA customers. Our expertise is in managing and delivering large, complex, and global transformation program.
Infor EAM
Birlasoft's strong capability on EAM offers global capabilities of consulting and technical services around EAM as part of the Infor solution suite. As part of the Infor EAM suite along with ERP suite, Birlasoft is engaged with its customers across the globe in delivering value added services.
