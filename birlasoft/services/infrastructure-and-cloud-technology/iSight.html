The need for zero touch, yet personalized and uninterrupted IT services is imperative. Businesses need UX led, intelligent, analytics-driven platforms to deliver business mandated IT objectives in line with evolving workstyles and dynamic business needs.

Birlasoft iSight platform is built to Discover, Monitor, Automate, Transform and Manage the Operations for IT Service Management, Cloud Management, Digital Workplace Automation, Asset monitoring, Alarm management, Multilingual bots and Unified Cyber Defense. Fully customizable and integrable with wide range of technology tools, iSight is a SaaS-based, AI-driven platform. The platform provides a single‐pane‐of‐glass with cognitive capabilities across multi cloud orchestration and automation, analytics and reporting, predictive IT operations, enabling digital bots for workforce and a cloud cost optimization engine to deliver IT service.

Business Benefits
All Pervasive, full spectrum IT Management platform
iSight helps reduce outage, improve agent productivity, achieve desired SLAs by automating ticket workflows through live chat enabled support. It effectively integrates, manages and monitors Apps, requests/incidents through mobile applications and generates intelligent analytics reports which help in decision making through a single console.
Unleash cloud capabilities for your business
iSight's Cloud Management platform helps enterprises accelerate cloud adoption and power innovation by allowing seamless integration with NextGen technologies. It effectively manages hybrid, multi-cloud environments to increase business agility and scalability
Designed for the Workforce of the future
iSight's through workplace automation techniques, provides context-aware, user-centric, personalized experience for the workforce of the future. It enables greater workforce productivity through effective collaboration and seamless communication and delivers superior end user experience.
Deeper Insights, greater visibility across Enterprise IT
iSight is designed to monitor & manage full spectrum of IT Infrastructure, including Datacenters, Servers, Networks, Devices and Applications. It's modern, integrated approach to IT Ops Management with deeper and meaningful insights provides real time visibility into alerts which helps minimize downtime and maximize performance. Through proactive threat-detection and automated response, iSight assures IT infrastructure security.
