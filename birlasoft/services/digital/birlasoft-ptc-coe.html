Birlasoft launches its First Center of Excellence for PTC Technology in the U.S.
We recently launched the first Center of Excellence (CoE) showcasing PTC technology in the US, at Birlasoft Raleigh, North Carolina location. The CoE focuses on demonstrating how customers can build great customer experiences on the PTC ThingWorx® Industrial Innovation Platform using Birlasoft accelerators.
It is the first of its kind in the US, and the second CoE that PTC and Birlasofthave collaborated on globally. Customers participating in the CoE launch experienced how Birlasoft has enabled faster time to market by creating a closed loop manufacturing cycle between engineering, product manufacturing, supply chain and service using ThingWorx.
