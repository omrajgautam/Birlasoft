The manually intensive commercial lines insurance underwriting needs to be transformed
Manually managing data from ACORD forms or broker emails, going through piles of paperwork, working on multiple systems, assessing exposures, seeking clearances, and tracking email exchanges with brokers and customers can hurt your business.
Don’t let an inefficient process negatively impact risk assessments, pricing, and efficacy.

Commercial Lines Submission Automation
For Lightning-fast, Efficient, and Profitable Underwriting
How Does It Work?
Uses AI and NLP to extract data from structured and unstructured sources like ACORD forms, spreadsheets, loss runs, and brokers emails.
This solution then applies automation and AI to help underwriters collaborate effectively, make faster and accurate risk decisions.
Submission Ingestion
AI and OCR help in extracting structured an unstructured data from different sources be it PDFs, spreadsheets, broker forms, and emails.
Submission Triage and Prioritization
Automation helps in managing separate submission queues for new business, renewals, and endorsements.
ML models help easily sort through hundreds of submissions to prioritize optimal entries based on risk appetite and underwriting triage guidelines.
Submission Enrichment
Real-time communication between systems to retrieve data for accurate and efficient underwriting decisioning.
Automation led data prefill in the quote setup of the policy system helps bind and issue of the policy.
AI & ML-led underwriting decisioning
Real-time communication between systems to retrieve data for accurate and efficient underwriting decisioning.
