Driving Digital Transformation across Life Sciences Value Chain
 
Leveraging our expertise with the best Digital Technology Platforms and Solutions, Birlasoft helps clients innovate faster to deliver more effective therapeutic products and services, reduce real-world costs, improve customer experience and meet global quality and regulatory compliance needs in a business efficient manner.
Birlasoft provides digital transformation services spanning the entire value chain of Pharmaceutical and Medical Device companies - from discovery, trials, and production to sales and marketing, field service and support.
 
Pharmaceutical and Medical Device companies must provide effective treatments for a broad range of healthcare issues while maintaining high product quality and controlling costs to be competitive. Significant M&A and divestiture activities in the industry necessitate agile corporate and IT strategies. Some of the trends driving significant changes in the Life Sciences and Healthcare industry include:
  • Evolving Customer Engagement Models
  • Better Transparency & Traceability Across the Supply Chain
  • Proliferation of Connected Devices
  • Increasingly Connected Patients
  • Global Regulatory Compliances
  • Growing Service - Centricity Across Medical Device Industry
Life Sciences companies need to adapt to the newer technological advancements that are taking place and focus on modernizing their existing systems and processes to navigate through these changes and capitalize on the opportunities. In order to differentiate their products and services in today's business climate, life sciences companies need to consider leveraging new technologies including Cloud, Mobility, Internet of Things (IoT), Big Data and Analytics, Product and Portfolio Management, Asset Management, Quality Management, Environmental Accounting and Reporting (sustainability), Modern marketing processes and solutions, CRM, and Modern Supply Chain solutions, among others.
